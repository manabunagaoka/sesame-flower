**Flower** is a family HOME system powered by Sesame Street - like Alexa/Echo Show but designed specifically for families with young children.

# ğŸ“± Feature Details

## ğŸŒ¸ CENTER: Flower AI Voice Assistant

**The heart of the app.** Flower is an AI companion that:

- Greets users when app opens
- Navigates between features via voice ("Show me videos")
- Answers questions in kid-friendly way
- Guides routines ("Time to brush your teeth!")
- Speaks in user's selected language (English, Spanish, Chinese)

**Technology Stack:**
- **STT:** Groq Whisper (ultra-fast transcription)
- **LLM:** OpenAI GPT-4o-mini (conversational AI)
- **TTS:** ElevenLabs (custom Flower voice)

# ğŸ¨ Design Principles

1. **Character Buttons, Not Emoji**
   - Use Sesame Street character images
   - Better branding than generic emoji
   - Kids recognize characters instantly

2. **Mobile-First**
   - Design for phone portrait mode
   - Tablet = larger version, same layout
   - Touch-friendly buttons (min 44px)

3. **Parent-First, Kid-Friendly**
   - Parents configure and control
   - Kids can use safely
   - Parental controls where needed

4. **Full-Screen Content**
   - Tap petal â†’ Full screen takeover
   - No distracting sidebars
   - Back gesture to return to flower

5. **Accessibility**
   - Voice-first for pre-readers
   - High contrast for visibility
   - Simple, clear iconography
